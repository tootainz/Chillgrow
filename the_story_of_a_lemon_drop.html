The Story of a Lemon Drop

Wednesday 05.01.2022

In 2021 I got excited about gardening and growing things. I was a little late with peppers however. I still decided to start a few pepper seeds and was very excited. All went well and soon i had two little seedlings. One day while checking my plants was devasteted to find out that my pepper seedlings were damping off from the ground up. I got angry and decided that i would show that i can still succsessfully grow a pepper plant. So i bought some lemon drop seeds and planted them in june. So the journey of my lemon drop begun.

With love, Joe.
